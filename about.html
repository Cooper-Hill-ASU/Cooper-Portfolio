Bio

My name is Cooper! I am 23, a STEM enthusiast, and transgender man from Chicago! Throughout my childhood, I frequented the Museum Campus in Chicago, and these visits made me fall in love with science and math. I was always a strong learner, and I always looked to use my abilities to help others throughout school and beyond.

Alongside math and science, I also have a deep appreciation for the arts. I write, draw, cook, and craft in my free time. Also, due to my studies, I also have plans to develop more sites and use my new coding abilities to create unique, artistic pieces for other projects.

Currently, I tutor students at my local Mathnasium. I teach children concepts ranging from number identification to calculus. My drive is seeing the moment students truly grasp a concept that once challenged them. Expanding knowledge, encouraging lifelong learning, and overcoming what once was thought to be impossible are feats I aspire to achieve with folks of all ages time and time again!
